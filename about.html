Our Journey

The Orbiscope Story

From Vision to Industry Leadership

Orbiscope was founded in 2008 by a team of geomatics engineers and GIS specialists who recognized the growing need for advanced spatial data solutions. What began as a small consultancy has grown into a comprehensive geospatial services provider with offices across Canada and the United States.

Our initial focus on municipal GIS solutions quickly expanded to include remote sensing, LiDAR processing, 3D modeling, and specialized geospatial analysis for various industries. This growth has been driven by our commitment to technical excellence, client satisfaction, and continuous innovation.

Today, Orbiscope is recognized as an industry leader in geomatics, trusted by government agencies, private corporations, and research institutions to provide accurate, insightful, and actionable geospatial intelligence.

Our Approach & Values

At Orbiscope, we believe that geospatial data is only valuable when it leads to better decisions. Our approach combines technical precision with practical application, ensuring that our solutions address real-world challenges and create tangible benefits for our clients.

Our core values guide everything we do:

  • Technical Excellence: We maintain the highest standards in geomatics engineering and spatial analysis
  • Innovation: We continuously explore new technologies and methodologies
  • Client Partnership: We work collaboratively with clients to understand their needs
  • Integrity: We deliver honest, transparent, and ethical service
  • Environmental Responsibility: We support sustainable development through our geospatial solutions
